About Me

I believe the best technology amplifies human creativity rather than replacing it. As a Senior Product Manager at Posit (formerly RStudio), I spend my days working with leading cloud partners like AWS, Databricks, and Snowflake to deliver enterprise product integrations that meet customers where they are. I also build AI systems that handle the routine work so teams can focus on creative problem solving instead of tedious task management. Over 10+ years in this space, I've learned that great product management isn't about the technology, it's about understanding the humans who use it. So while AI is exciting and certainly has my attention, my purpose has never been more clear: to find my own little way to improve what it means to be human.

Work
